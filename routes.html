Strava, Ride With GPS, and locals shops are great ways to find routes

Where should you ride?

Use Strava Use Ride With GPS Ask at Local Bike Shops
You can use Strava to find bike routes by exploring popular segments and activities shared by other cyclists in your area, or by searching for recommended routes using the Strava Explore feature. You can use Ride With GPS to find bike routes by searching for routes shared by other cyclists, browsing featured rides, or using the route planner to create your own custom route You can ask at local bike shops for recommendations on the best cycling routes in the area, as staff often have expert knowledge and can suggest rides suited to your interests and skill level.
