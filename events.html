Events and Group Rides.

Southern Vermont has a vibrant cycling community, and there are many events and group rides you can join. Here are some options:

  • Thursday night rides with Burrows Sports: Info available from the shop's Facebook page.

  • The Hungry Lion Bike Tour: A great fundraiser held during foliage season. Help raise money to support local foodbanks by participating in one of several options. Information here.
  • The Retreat Farm Ramble: A fun ride that takes place in the fall, with options for all levels of riders. More info here.
