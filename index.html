I've been cycling in Vermont for just a short time. An e-bike was my gateway drug, and now I ride completely under my own power.

Vermont is a cyclist's dream.

Vermont offers a wide range of cycling opportunities, from scenic road rides to challenging mountain trails. The state's diverse terrain and beautiful landscapes make it a perfect destination for cyclists of all levels. Whether you're looking for leisurely rides along the lakes or thrilling adventures in the Green Mountains, Vermont has something to offer every cycling enthusiast.

Vermont is known for its picturesque landscapes, charming small towns, and vibrant cycling community.

  • There are plenty of quiet paved roads for the road cyclist
  • Gravel riders will find a seemingly endless range of opportunities, from well maintained hard packed dirt roads, to roughard packed dirt roads, to rough and rocky double track, or gnarly unmaintained class 4 roads.
  • Mountain bikers will find a wide range of trails, from beginner to expert, and everything in between.
